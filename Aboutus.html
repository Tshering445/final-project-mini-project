About Us

Managed by dedicated mess coordinators and supported by the college administration, the mess operates efficiently with a team of cooks working in shifts. Additionally, student counselors manage weekly shopping, maintain transparency by presenting detailed bills, and allocate stipends effectively to meet studentsâ€™ needs. Students can choose between dining in the mess or preparing their own meals using fully-equipped kitchens on campus, promoting flexibility and self-sufficiency.

Our Vision

To create a model mess management system that ensures transparency, quality, and efficiency in delivering meals while fostering student leadership, self-sufficiency, and healthy dietary practices.

Our Mission

- To provide high-quality, hygienic, and nutritious meals to students.
- To ensure transparency in mess operations through detailed financial management and reporting.
- To empower students by involving them in the management of the mess, fostering leadership and accountability.
- To continuously enhance the mess facilities to meet the diverse needs and preferences of the student body.
